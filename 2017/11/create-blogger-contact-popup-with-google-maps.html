The function of the contact form on a blog is to make it easier for blog visitors to contact the blog admin.

The function of the contact form on a blog is to make it easier for blog visitors to contact the blog admin.&nbsp;Blogger itself has provided a contact form that is already connected with the Gmail account admin blog.&nbsp;

Well this time I will share how to create a contact form Blogger popup with Google Maps equipped, so this (Google Maps) will be more to give visitors trust to the blog admin, especially if the blog is used for business or online store.&nbsp;Visitors or consumers will be more confident because the business location is clear.&nbsp;
