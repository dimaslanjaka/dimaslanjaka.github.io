Bluestacks Game Control The Legend Of Neverland

Game control the legend of neverland bluestacks Support change fairy and job skill

Requirements

  • Bluestacks (4 or 5 supported) ( Download Bluestacks )

Features

  • Switch Fairies
    • Team 1 (CTRL+Z)
    • Team 2 (CTRL+X)
    • Team 3 (CTRL+C)
    • Team 4 (CTRL+V)
  • Switch Jobs/Classes
    • Job 1 (CTRL+A)
    • Job 2 (CTRL+S)
    • Job 3 (CTRL+D)
  • Zoom in (-) - Zoom out (+)
  • Rotate Camera Using D-PAD Arrows
  • 2 Skill Fairies (E & Q)
  • And More
