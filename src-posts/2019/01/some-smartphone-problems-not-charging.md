charged for long.</span></span> </p>  <div></div>  <p style="text-align: justify;"> <span class="notranslate"> <span>To overcome this we can calibrate the battery.</span></span> </p>  <p class="notranslate"> <span>Do not charge the smartphone and let the battery run out until he dies himself.</span></span> </li>  <li style="font-weight: 400;"> <span class="notranslate"> <span>Turn it on and let your smartphone turn itself off again.</span></span> </li>  <li style="font-weight: 400;"> <span class="notranslate"> <span>Charge the smartphone off until the indicator shows a level of
